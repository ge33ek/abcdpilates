About abcd PILATES

I believe with the appropriate guidance and support,  any body can do PILATES. Pilates is a unique mind-body exercise method that is accessible to everyone. Any age, any gender, any size, any profession, any fitness level, I can teach you.

 

Have you heard of Pilates and think you can’t do it?

I will show you that you can.

any body can do PILATES

 

As Joseph Pilates said -

"In 10 sessions you will feel the difference

In 20 sessions you will see the difference

In 30 sessions you will have a whole new body"

With the latest models of STOTT PILATES® equipment by Merrithew Corporation imported from Canada: a V2 Max Plus Reformer with full vertical frame, Split Pedal Stability Chair, Ladder Barrel, Arc Barrels, Jumpboard and fun accessories. You will have the full Pilates experience in an environment that is beautiful, calm, clean, filled with natural light and welcoming.
