About Jeannie-Marie Noyce

I am a warm, passionate, gentle and motivating Pilates instructor, who brings patience, empathy and precision to every client session. I am trained and certified in the STOTT PILATES® method of exercise.

 

I discovered Pilates, following a car accident that resulted in spinal injury.  The practice of Pilates worked miracles in healing my body. It was a significant part of the process of rebuilding my body. Continued practice of Pilates keeps me pain-free, medication-free, flexible, toned, healthy and strong. You will find that even just 20 minutes of Pilates makes you feel calmer, stronger, more balanced, a little lighter, perhaps even a little taller.

 

I have made substantial investment into cultivating my expertise in Pilates. My pursuit of teaching and training opportunities has taken me to Hong Kong, Singapore, Sydney, Melbourne, London and New York. I have also taken Pilates classes in 15 countries. This global exposure to a range of methods and teaching styles of practicing Pilates has made me a well-rounded and competent instructor.

 

Prior to becoming a Pilates instructor I spent a decade working in corporate law and finance. My analytical style and attention to detail gives me a great eye for assessing an individual’s needs.
